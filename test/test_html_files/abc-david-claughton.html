David Claughton

David Claughton was born in Perth and has a communications degree from Murdoch University. He began his career as a breakfast announcer in community radio. He has travelled widely and went with his family as an Australian volunteer to Nicaragua. David started with the ABC in 2000 as a rural reporter in Mount Gambier, South Australia and became executive producer of the NSW Country Hour in 2005.
